Hello!

I’m a Digital Media Expert, currently finishing a PhD in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) in Nottingham (UK).

In my PhD, I have been developing tools, methods and processes to design complex cross-media experiences, supported by HCI theoretical knowledge.

My skills span project management, user experience design (UXD), information architecture (IA) and full-stack web development.
